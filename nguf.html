    NEVER GIVE UP FOUNDATION

    During my internship at Studio C, I was tasked with helping make some of the mock-ups for the Never Give Up Foundation, a non-profit organization made up of cause-based staff and volunteers striving to bridge the gap between children with learning disabilities, their parents, their schools, and businesses. Though I had a lighter role in the completion of this particular project, I still provided design aid and general advice to my peers.
