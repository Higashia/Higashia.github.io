    SHARDS

    A personal project of mine, Shards was one of my much more recent works. A high fantasy oriented project, Shards was meant to feel very dark and somewhat mysterious. As such, i prompted to use a duller color scheme and mute many of the images, allowing the actual text and other bits of information stand out and fully contrast with everything else, I even went about adding a soft white glow effect, to further highlight these particular aspects.
